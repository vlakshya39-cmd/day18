01

Summary

The leadership team reviewed Q2 performance against the annual growth target. Revenue grew 12%, short of the 18% goal, mainly because 5 of 14 expected enterprise contracts slipped past the quarter due to procurement delays and added security reviews. Marketing reported a 34% rise in website traffic but weaker conversion, prompting a planned redesign that Engineering flagged as a capacity risk. Customer Success noted strong 94% retention alongside a significant rise in support tickets, both tied to reporting performance and dashboard loading times — an issue Engineering is already investigating and the group agreed to prioritize. Hiring decisions and the annual customer conference budget were both postponed pending further inputs, and the team agreed not to publicly commit to the August analytics dashboard date until a milestone review is complete.
